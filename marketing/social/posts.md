## Free Distribution Checklist

- Post consistently on WhatsApp Status, Facebook, Instagram and TikTok.
- Ask local community-group administrators before sharing promotional posts and follow each group's rules.
- Send the driver post directly to verified taxi and delivery contacts instead of mass messaging strangers.
- Ask participating businesses to repost the business graphic and tag Lin Ride.
- Encourage passengers to share the website after a successful trip.
- Never publish passenger details, trip locations, phone numbers or driver documents.
- Get permission before using a person's name, face, vehicle or testimonial.
- Do not promise driver availability, guaranteed pickup times or discounts that Lin Ride cannot yet provide.
